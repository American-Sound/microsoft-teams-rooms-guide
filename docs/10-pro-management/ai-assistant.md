# AI Assistant in PMP

## Overview

The AI Assistant is accessible via an icon in the top-right banner of the PMP portal. Currently in **Public Preview** (commercial cloud only; not available in GCC, GCC-H, or sovereign clouds).

## Key Facts

- **No additional license required** — this is not Copilot. No Copilot license needed.
- Requires Teams Rooms Pro license and one of: Global Administrator, Teams Administrator, or Teams Rooms Pro Manager role
- **Provides information only; does not perform actions**

## Capabilities

**Knowledge queries:** How-to questions about Teams meeting spaces and management, feature comparisons, configuration guidance using latest documentation.

**Data queries:** Extract data about room Health and Inventory using natural language. Results are scoped to the user's role assignment and room scope.

### Example Prompts

- "How can I create a role for my staff that has permissions to reports and tickets?"
- "What is the cause of the Meeting app version going unhealthy?"
- "Show me how many rooms were enrolled in the last two weeks, broken down by manufacturers and locations"
- "What is the difference between Autopilot and One-time passcode?"

The assistant interacts in the language configured for the portal (automatic translation). Users can thumbs-up/thumbs-down each response for quality feedback.
